Common questions
Who can apply for a mortgage in Italy?
Anyone residing in Italy can apply for a mortgage, regardless of nationality. Requirements vary by employment type, income, and the property. We assess your profile and find the right solution.
How long does the mortgage process take in Italy?
Typically between 6 and 10 weeks from application to signing — depending on the bank and how quickly documents are collected. We work to keep it as fast as possible.
What documents will I need?
The standard documents include: valid ID, tax code (codice fiscale), recent payslips or income proof, bank statements, and the property details. We send you a complete checklist after your first call.
What are your fees?
Our advisory fee is discussed transparently during your first call, based on the complexity of your situation and loan amount. There are no hidden charges — everything is agreed upfront before we start working.
